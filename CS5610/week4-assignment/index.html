Input

  • Mouse — Logitech G Pro Wireless (GPW): 2.12 oz (60 g), neutral shape that stays comfortable through long sessions.
  • Microphone — Blue Yeti: Logitech USB setup with clear voice pickup and easy gain control.
  • Controller — FeiZhi Vader 3: responsive sticks and confident triggers for racers and action games.
  • Keyboard — Wooting 80HE: magnetic switches with analog control, with a custom zinc base, and Rapid Trigger for snappy resets
    • Analog movement smooths strafing and peeking
    • Short throws help with quick taps and rhythm play

Output

  1. Left monitor — Alienware AW2518HF: 1080p at 240 Hz verticle showing for coding and browering.
  2. Center monitor — ASUS VG27AQML1A: 2k up to 260 Hz; my main screen for mixes of work and play.
  3. Right monitor — BenQ XL2546K: 240 Hz competitive screen, now for side help infomation display.
  4. Speakers — Bose: easy near-field placement with warm mids for music and calls, with bluetooth and 3.5mm connections.
